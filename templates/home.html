About Us

Welcome to Chef's Burger Samakhusi

We opened Chef Burger Samakhusi for our customers on 2020. We are small family and friends run business. Since we open, we have served many customers and we are delighted to get lots of good reviews. Those beautiful words are always inspiration for us. We always do our best to satisfy our customers.

We endeavour to offer wide and varied choices of the best Nepalese cuisines as well as classic Indian dishes. Traditional cuisines created in contemporary manner with emphasis of healthy eating. Our chefs will prepare each dish with fresh ingredients, low fat & significant reduction in-food colouring. Our chefs are renowned for their speciality dishes. And our contemporary venue is always there for you with our courteous & friendly staff.
